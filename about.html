Seems Fair is a space for looking closely at the systems that shape our everyday lives.

I’m an AI researcher with a background in computational social choice, interested in bias, fairness, and AI ethics. Here, I use tools from theory and data analysis to explore and question how rules, models, and decision-making processes influence real-world outcomes... often in ways we don’t notice at first glance.

Many systems seem fair, until you look a little closer.
